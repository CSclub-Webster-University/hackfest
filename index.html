THE 300-POINT SCALE

To win, teams must excel across all three disciplines. Every project is evaluated on a 300-point scale.

100 POINTS

Technical Excellence

Host: Computer Science Club

Evaluated on code architecture, complexity, performance, and stability. We look for efficient systems, clean logic, and robust technical implementation.

100 POINTS

Creative Design

Host: Game Design Club

Evaluated on narrative cohesion, aesthetic quality, and "game feel" (UX). We look for immersive worlds, intuitive interfaces, and satisfying interaction loops.

100 POINTS

Strategic Excellence

Host: Business Club

Evaluated on market fit, monetization strategy, and the final pitch. We look for viable business models, clear value propositions, and professional presentation.

HOW IT WORKS

STAGE 01

THE INITIALIZATION

Friday Kickoff

The journey begins at 5:30 PM. We reveal the Secret Theme, provide Problem Statements, and release the list of Diversifiers. This is where you find your crew, pick your challenge, and plan your strategy.

STAGE 02

THE COMPILATION

The Weekend Sprint

This is the core grind. Your team has until Sunday afternoon to develop a functional prototype (Solution or Game) AND a robust business model. Use this time to iterate, test, and polish both the tech and the pitch.

STAGE 03

THE DEPLOYMENT

Sunday Finale

At 2:30 PM, the code is locked. Your project will be reviewed by a panel of judges who will evaluate it according to the criteria mentioned above.
